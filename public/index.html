So, who am I?

I am a passionate Mechatronics Engineer specializing in Embedded Systems, Robotics, and Industrial Automation. My expertise lies in bridging hardware and software to develop cyber-physical systems, optimize industrial processes, and innovate in embedded applications.

Currently, I work as a Regional Automation Process Engineer at Procter & Gamble, developing data-driven automation solutions and troubleshooting automated guided vehicles (AGVs). My past experiences include working on autonomous drones, industrial automation, and cloud-integrated cyber-physical systems.
